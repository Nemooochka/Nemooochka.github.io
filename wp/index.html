Las Vegas Life Experience® Details

Las Vegas aka “The Entertainment Capital of the World” is an amazing city known for its vibrant nightlife, centered around 24-hour casinos and virtually unlimited entertainment options.

You will be staying at the Famous Planet Hollywood Resort and Casino.  Planet Hollywood is located on the Las Vegas Strip. It is a modern hotel with a casino, spa, fitness center, and newly renovated rooms.  Plenty of restaurants to choose from which you can dine like a movie star and maybe even spot one of your favorite celebrities. Several shows to choose from with its 3 theaters.  The hotel also has The Miracle Mile shops with over 170 stores for your shopping fun.

Las Vegas is known for its world famous “All You Can Eat” buffets filled with endless choices of cuisine! You won’t be going hungry on this Life Experience® because we’ve included a pass that allows you to choose where you want to eat. You can enjoy breakfast, lunch, dinner or satisfy any cravings in between at the Planet Hollywood Spice Market Buffet, Rio Carnival World Buffet, Paris Flavors Buffet, or the Flamingo Paradise Garden Buffet or Caesars Palace Buffet!

On your free time, visit the best Las Vegas has to offer like the breathtaking views from the 108th floor of the Stratosphere. Or try your luck at one of the world’s premier casinos, you might just walk away with the jackpot! With its amazing exhibitions, spectacular shows, and exotic cuisine, this Life Experience® will create unique and unforgettable memories! But remember… “What Happens in Vegas, Stays in Vegas!”

Itinerary

Day 1 Arrive in Las Vegas

We will provide transportation to the Planet Hollywood Resort. You will be greeted and provided with the designated place and time for the Welcome meeting. Be sure to attend the Welcome Meeting as we will provide all of the details of this Life Experience®, trip! You will enjoy a buffet dinner and drinks while you mingle with your fellow Life Experience® travelers. After getting acquainted and receiving your itinerary for the next few days, you can enjoy the hotel and what Las Vegas has to offer.

Day 2Excursion to Hoover Dam

After a hearty breakfast at the buffet get ready to take in the breathtaking sights at the Hoover Dam. You will be picked up between 8-9am and then board one of our Luxury Motor Coach buses. There is free Wifi, charging outlets, and TV’s for your entertainment on board. At the dam you will have access to the Bypass Bridge, Visitors Center, Exhibit Halls, Museum, & Movie Room. You will be amazed at the breathtaking views and vistas from the Hover Dam and leave knowing why it is one of the 8 Wonders of the World!

Finish your day by indulging in Vegas’ dazzling attractions on your free time! Wherever you turn, The Strip flickers seductively with its promise of excitement. Walk the streets of Paris and take a selfie by the Fountains of Bellagio. Share cocktails with an impressive view of the city in the 107th floor of the Stratosphere’s SkyLounge. Before heading back to the hotel, enjoy some lobster and shrimp frittata at the Tableau.

Day 3Free Day

Your last day in the city that never sleeps! Remember you will have a full day pass for the buffets while you are out enjoying your day and evening in Las Vegas.

Day 4Departure Day

Like all good things, it must come to an end. Be sure to take some photos with all your new friends from around the world as you depart. Hotel check-out time is 11:00 AM. Be sure to plan on leaving the hotel 3 hours prior to your scheduled flight departure time. If you end up being there a little bit early you can stock up on some last minute souvenirs.

If your departure is not until later in the day, the front desk staff will be happy to securely store your belongings so that you can head out and snap a few more “Making Wishes Real” photos in Las Vegas. Remember, even if you get a notice that your flight has been delayed, you’ll still need to be at the airport at the same time as per your original schedule, sometimes the airlines will suddenly find a replacement plane and you don’t want to be left behind.

But then again, maybe you do!
